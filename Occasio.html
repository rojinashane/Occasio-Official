Getting Started

How to Install Occasio on Android

Occasio is an Android app distributed as an APK. Follow these simple steps to download and install it on your phone.

1
Tap the Download Button
Press Download for Android above or the Get APK button in the menu. The .apk file will begin downloading to your phone's storage.
2
Open Your Downloads
Once the download finishes, open your Files or Downloads app and tap on the Occasio.apk file to begin installation.
3
Allow Unknown Sources
Android will ask for permission to install apps from outside the Play Store. Tap Settings, then toggle on "Allow from this source" — then go back and tap Install.
4
Install the App
Tap Install on the confirmation screen. The installation takes just a few seconds. Do not close the prompt.
5
Open Occasio & Get Started
Once installed, tap Open — or find the Occasio icon on your home screen. Create an account and start planning your first event in Sorsogon!
Compatibility: Occasio requires Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher. If you encounter any issues during installation, make sure your device has enough storage and that the APK file was fully downloaded.
